About

Here's a little about Are You Ready? 2013

‘Are You Ready?’ organized by the Rotaract Club of University of Moratuwa in collaboration with the Career Guidance Unit of University of Moratuwa is the main event in the university calendar which adorns its careers’ week. This year's event is the 18th consecutive ‘Are You Ready?’ programme that is being organized since its inception in 1998.
